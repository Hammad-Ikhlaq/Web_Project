We use big data to accelerate journey towards carbon net zero

MilkyWay owners are already among the most climate friendly dairy farmers in the world, producing milk with less than half the average of emissions per litre compared to global dairy production. Now MilkyWay is introducing a new Climate Check programme and comprehensive support to all its 9,900 farmer owners in Pakistan to fight climate impact. This will pave the way for the company to reach its ambition to reduce carbon emission by 30 per cent by 2030 and have a neutral climate impact by 2050.
